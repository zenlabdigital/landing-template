Privacy Policy

1. Private Data We Receive and Collect

ZenLab Digital automatically collects and receives certain information from your computer or mobile device, including the activities you perform on our Website, the Platforms, and the Applications, the type of hardware and software you are using (for example, your operating system or browser), and information obtained through your interactions with our services.

1.1. Each Time You Visit the Website

For example, each time you visit https://www.zenlab.digital/ or otherwise use our Services, we automatically collect your IP address, browser and device type, access times, the web page from which you came, the regions from which you navigate the web page, and the web page(s) you access. This information helps us provide a secure and personalized experience.

1.2. When You First Register for a ZenLab Digital Account

We collect your billing address and necessary information to complete financial transactions. When purchasing through our Services, we may collect your credit card or PayPal information. This sensitive data is handled with the highest security standards and is not shared with third parties not involved in the process.

2. How We Use Your Information

ZenLab Digital uses visitors' data for specific purposes that are essential to providing our technology services. We use this information to identify you when you login and maintain security throughout your session.

We verify your transactions for purchase confirmation, billing, security, and authentication (including security tokens for communication). We implement robust security measures to protect your data when using our software development, VPS, and AI services.

  • To identify you when you login to your account and protect your session while using our development services, VPS, or AI tools
  • To enable us to operate our Services securely and provide seamless technology solutions without compromising your confidential information
  • To verify your transactions and ensure secure authentication across all our digital services and products

Communication and Customer Service

We share aggregate (non-identifiable) statistics about service usage with our partners. We keep you informed about our Services, features, newsletters, offers, surveys, and events we think you may find useful. We may use this data to improve our software development services, VPS offerings, and AI solutions.

3. Understanding and Improving Our Services

We analyze aggregated data to diagnose problems, analyze trends, improve our features, and better understand our users' needs. This helps us maintain secure and efficient services for everyone, from administrators to regular users.

3.1. Tracking Technologies

ZenLab Digital uses tracking technology on our website and platforms, including mobile application identifiers and unique user IDs. This helps us recognize you across different Services, monitor usage, and customize your experience.

3.2. User Agreement

By visiting https://www.zenlab.digital/ or using our Services, you agree to the collection of information as described in this policy. This includes automatic collection of device information, access times, and navigation data.

  • Security Measures: We implement comprehensive security protocols to protect your data across all our services
  • Service Optimization: We continuously improve our software development, VPS, and AI services based on usage patterns
  • Data Protection: We ensure your information is protected during transactions and service usage

4. Data Sharing and Statistical Analysis

We may share anonymized statistical data to improve our services and understand market trends. This helps us enhance our technology solutions and provide better service to our customers.

Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us at info@zenlab.digital or visit https://www.zenlab.digital/. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparent information about our data practices.
